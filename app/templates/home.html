A new design for mental health

The modern age has produced many ways to treat mental illness, from talk therapy to first generation digital therapeutics. But new approaches are needed to meet the soaring global demand for accessible solutions that can help people manage symptoms in the moment, and over a lifetime. We’re on a mission to meet the need for a new generation of therapeutics that are as dynamic as the people they serve and the world in which we live. Digital products designed to easily adapt to symptoms and severity, and deliver the right intervention to the right person at the right time.
